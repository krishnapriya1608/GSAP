PRINCE OF PERSIA

Prince of Persia" is a classic video game franchise that has been beloved by gamers since its inception. It was originally created by Jordan Mechner and first released in 1989. The game is known for its pioneering use of rotoscoping, a technique that involves tracing over motion picture footage to create realistic animations.

Need For Speed

"Need for Speed" (NFS) is a popular racing video game franchise developed by Ghost Games (formerly by Criterion Games and Black Box) and published by Electronic Arts. The series has been a major player in the racing genre since its debut in 1994.

GTA

GTA, or Grand Theft Auto, is a popular video game series developed by Rockstar Games. It's known for its open-world gameplay, where players can explore massive cities, engage in various criminal activities, and interact with a dynamic environment.


The series is famous for its detailed storytelling, satirical humor, and the freedom it offers players.
